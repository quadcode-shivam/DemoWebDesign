ABOUT US
The LearningHUB is a free, online Literacy and Basic Skills (LBS) program for English speaking adults living in Ontario. As an online program, we are here to make learning accessible! We help adults who live in communities with limited services cannot attend in-person LBS programs want to learn on the computer
